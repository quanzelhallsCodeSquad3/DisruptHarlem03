Angelina Jolie in the movie "Salt"

Evelyn Salt, played by Angelina Jolie, is a CIA agent found out to be a Russian spy whose mission is to assassinate the Russian President and the American President to unltimately start a war.

Salt, as she goes by in the movie, decides to abort her original mission after she finds her husband, the man she married for a cover in her in mission but fell in love with, killed by Orlov her Russian defector.
